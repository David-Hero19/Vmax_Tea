Our Story

Started with two flasks and a folding table.

VMAX began in 2021 as a small stall serving hot tea to students on their way to early classes. Today we're a full shop, but the goal hasn't changed: pour something warm, ask how your day is going, and mean it.

FAQ

Quick answers.

Do you deliver?

Not yet through the site directly — order through our WhatsApp and we'll arrange delivery nearby, or pick up in-store.

Can I reserve a table?

Yes — use the reservation form above, or message us on WhatsApp with your date, time, and party size.

Do you make custom cakes?

We take custom cake orders with a bit of notice — reach out on WhatsApp with what you have in mind.

What payment methods are accepted?

Card and bank transfer online at checkout (via Paystack), or cash and transfer in-store.
